A social proof section is used to build trust by showing real evidence that other people already use, like, or recommend a product or service. Instead of the website just saying “we are good,” it shows reviews, ratings, testimonials, or user numbers so visitors feel more confident and less unsure.

It works because people naturally follow what others have already approved. When users see that many people had a positive experience, they are more likely to believe it is safe and useful.
